Jakub Tomczak

I am an assistant professor of Artificial Intelligence in the Computational Ingelligence group (led by Prof. A.E. Eiben) at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. I was a deep learning researcher (Engineer, Staff) in Qualcomm AI Rersearch in Amsterdam (10/2018 - 12/2019). Previously, I was a Marie Sklodowska-Curie fellow in Max Welling's group at University of Amsterdam. My main research interests include deep learning, Bayesian inference and deep generative modeling.
